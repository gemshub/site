    GEMS: Gibbs Energy Minimization Software for Geochemical Modeling

    • GEMS provides a versatile framework for modeling complex natural and engineered chemical systems involving many (non)ideal solid or liquid solutions, gas mixture or non-ideal gaseous fluids.

    • GEMS along with its related codes and thermodynamic databases, are essential scientific tools for thermodynamic modeling of chemical processes. These processes include aqueous speciation, solid-gas-liquid interactions, mineral dissolution/precipitation, kinetics, oxidation/reduction, and surface adsorption.

    • GEMS enables students, scientists, and engineers to learn and improve modeling skills, predict system behavior, optimize processes, and solve challenges in geochemistry, environmental science, waste disposal, material science, and chemical engineering.
